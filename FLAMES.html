Game Mechanics

The game involves crossing out common letters in the names of the two individuals and counting the remaining letters to determine the relationship outcome. It's often played for fun during leisure time or as a light-hearted activity during social gatherings.

1. Enter the names into both provided boxes.

2. Click the "calculate" button to determine the relationship between the entered names.

3. The names and their corresponding relationships will be displayed below.
